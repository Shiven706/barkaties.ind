About Us

BARKATIES is dedicated to empowering youth by securing and enhancing the education and healthcare sectors. Through innovative programs, community engagement, and strategic partnerships, we aim to create sustainable development.

Healthcare is vital for youth as it ensures their physical, mental, and emotional well-being. Access to quality healthcare services helps prevent and treat illnesses, enabling young individuals to lead healthy lives and achieve their full potential.

Education is crucial for youth as it equips them with essential knowledge and skills for personal and professional success. It empowers them to make informed decisions, pursue opportunities, and contribute positively to society
